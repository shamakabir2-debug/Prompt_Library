# 07 · Inventory Management and Stock Optimisation

## Prompt Text

> Act as an inventory management analyst for the ShopGO e-commerce platform. Analyse the following product data, current inventory levels, and demand forecasts to optimise stock management.
>
> Provide:
> 1. Stock status (Overstocked / Optimal / Low Stock / Out of Stock)  
> 2. Recommended action (e.g., reorder, reduce stock, maintain current level)  
> 3. Brief justification based on demand trends and inventory levels  
>
> If the data is incomplete or inconsistent, classify the result as **"Review Required"**.
>
> **Data inputs:**  
> Current inventory levels: [Insert stock data]  
> Demand forecast: [Insert forecast data]  
> Product details: [Insert category, sales rate, lead time]

## Intended Workflow or Task

This prompt is used to support inventory monitoring, replenishment decisions, and stock optimisation across the ShopGO platform.

## Problem Being Solved

Managing inventory across a large e-commerce platform is complex and error-prone. Poor inventory decisions can lead to overstocking, resulting in increased storage costs and waste, or stockouts, leading to lost sales and reduced customer satisfaction.

## Automation Potential

**High.**  
Inventory monitoring and recommendations can be automated using real-time data and demand forecasts. AI enables continuous tracking across large product ranges, reducing manual effort and improving decision consistency. Human oversight is required for strategic decisions and exceptions.

## Risks and Limitations

- Incorrect demand forecasts may lead to poor inventory decisions  
- Supply chain delays or sudden demand spikes may not be captured  
- AI may overlook strategic, seasonal, or contextual factors  
- Data inaccuracies can affect recommendation quality
